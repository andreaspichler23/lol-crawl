Fancy ARAM Matchhistory is a tool for displaying your personal ARAM history in a fancy and detailed way. It displays in-depths stats for each champion as well as tables with infos to all your played games. It has vast amount of interactivity and gives the consumer the possibility to analyse her/his own stats in different ways as well as to compare their stats to the average ARAM player. Fancy ARAM Matchhistory features interactive plots generated by python/Plotly/Dash. We are using the match API.
